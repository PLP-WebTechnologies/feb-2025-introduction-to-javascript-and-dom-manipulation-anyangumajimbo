About the Club

Who We Are

The TUK French Club is a vibrant student organization at the Technical University of Kenya, dedicated to promoting French language and culture.

Our Mission

We aim to enhance French language skills, encourage talent, and promote cultural exchange.

Visit our Our members for more information about the club members..

Activities and Achievements

What We Do

The TUK French Club organizes a variety of activities to enrich members' experiences. From French debates and theater performances to language camps and cultural events, we provide opportunities for students to practice French in fun and interactive ways.

Recent Achievements

We are proud of our members who have excelled in national competitions and participated in international programs like the Assistant de Langue initiative.
